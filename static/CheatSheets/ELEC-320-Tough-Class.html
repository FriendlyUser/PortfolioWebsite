Solving Abrupt Silicon PN Junction Question

  • 1. Calculate \(V_{bi}\).

  • 2. Look up \(D_p\) on the diffusion coefficient chart. 2.3

  • 3. Calculate the diffusion length: \(L_p = \sqrt {D_p \tau _p}\) (for \(p^+n\)) - or - \(L_n=\sqrt {D_n \tau _n}\) (for \(pn^+\))

  • 4. If: \(L_p > x_p \) (for \(p^+n\)) - or - \(L_n > x_n\) (for \(pn^+)\), the diode is short-base.
